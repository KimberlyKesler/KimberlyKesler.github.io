Aidan J Cardenas

Category:Branding

The Challenge: To create a full brand system for digital and print.

About the Project

Aidan J Cardenas is a multidisciplinary creative specializing in web development, photography, and media technology. From writing clean, functional code to managing live production systems, he brings technical expertise and artistic perspective to his work.

The identity developed for him reflects that synergy: structured yet creative, modern yet grounded, and designed to evolve alongside his growing body of work.

Visual Strategy

Mr. Cardenas wanted a brand identity that reflected his full range of skills in web dev and media without feeling disjointed. We developed a tech-forward visual direction using shades of red, blue, black, and white.

This process helped refine his ideas into a cohesive direction featuring clean, structured design elements and technology-inspired textures.

Identity Development

The logo began with inspiration from the straight lines of a circuit board. As the concept evolved, we integrated the structure of a camera lens shutter to reflect his photography work.

The final mark merges these ideas within a hexagon that reinforces precision and technical strength, creating a cohesive symbol for all aspects of his creative output.

Comprehensive Brand System

A complete system was developed featuring a defined typography hierarchy, a refined color palette, and custom iconography derived from the primary mark. I also developed light UX concepts to establish a digital visual direction.

The system was finalized with practical brand extensions, including a resume and business card, ensuring a professional identity across both digital and print platforms.
