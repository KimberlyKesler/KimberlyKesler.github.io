The Eventure Company

Category: Logo and Soft Branding

The Challenge: Develop a foundational logo with scalability for future brand expansion.

About the Project

The Eventure Company was founded by two professionals who share a passion for education, creativity, and community connection. Together, they created a community-based event system designed to bring people of all ages together through engaging, skill-building experiences.

Their mission is to plan and host a wide range of events from fine arts and STEM workshops to sports and community gatherings. Each event is designed to foster growth, creativity, and meaningful connection.

Visual Strategy

The mood board served as a centralized internal space to gather and refine visual ideas. It included imagery of art, classrooms, sports, and calendars to reflect the wide range of programming offered.

Arrows were incorporated as a motif symbolizing focus and forward momentum, while paint palette elements inspired a sense of color and creativity. This clarified a visual tone that is energetic yet structured before moving into development.

Identity Development

Exploration began with the concept of a folding desktop calendar, immediate in its association with scheduling, structure, and coordination.

As the concept developed, the triangular fold of the calendar resembled an arrow, reinforcing forward-thinking momentum. By simplifying the structure into geometric shapes, the mark maintains clarity while communicating movement and purpose.

Final Execution

The calendar form was angled to enhance the sense of movement, and the letter "E" was integrated to function as structured lines within the calendar grid. This creates a unified relationship between the symbol and typography.

The bold color palette was designed to attract children while remaining modern for adults. Developed with expansion in mind, the next phase includes social media templates and printable materials to support community engagement.
