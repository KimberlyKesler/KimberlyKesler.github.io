Flowers & Frosting Cake Co.

Category: Logo Design

The Challenge: Create a detailed, hand drawn logo that blends baked goods and florals into one cohesive identity.

About the Project

Flowers & Frosting Cake Co. is a small batch bakery rooted in tradition, creativity, and family. Inspired by her daughters and a lifelong love of baking, the owner creates beautifully detailed cakes and handcrafted treats that feel both nostalgic and personal.

From custom celebration cakes to seasonal confections, each creation reflects care, craftsmanship, and a deep appreciation for meaningful detail. Her work transforms everyday baked goods into thoughtful, story-driven pieces.

Visual Strategy

The mood board was built directly from the clientâ€™s own work, showcasing her cakes, cookies, textures, and warm color palette. Rich browns, marigold tones, soft pinks, and muted greens reflected both her baked goods and the floral inspiration tied to her daughters.

The client requested the South Holland typeface and wanted it integrated organically into the mark. A hand-drawn aesthetic was essential to mirror the artisanal nature of her baking. The overall direction embraced detail, texture, and warmth to align with her intricate cake designs and inviting brand personality.

Design Development

Early exploration focused on how to merge a chocolate chunk cookie with floral elements intentionally. Refinement centered on organizing details so the logo felt full yet balanced. The cookie needed to remain recognizable while allowing space for marigolds, hazelnuts, and greenery to coexist harmoniously.

Line work was adjusted to maintain a hand-drawn quality without losing structure, and the South Holland lettering was carefully curved along the form to unify the composition. Both full color and black and white variations were developed to ensure versatility across print, packaging, and digital watermarking.

Final Execution

The final logo blends a bitten chocolate chunk cookie with a flourishing bouquet of marigolds, greenery, and hazelnut accents. The curved script softens the structure and enhances the handcrafted feel, reflecting the artistry found in her baked creations.

The result is a distinctive identity that captures the heart of Flowers & Frosting Cake Co., celebrating family, tradition, and the beauty found in thoughtful detail.
