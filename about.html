ABOUT ME

Hey y’all. Ever since I was a child, I have always been drawn to creativity. Whether it was creating custom pictures for my friends or selling stationery sets door to door, I knew that my passion for design would shape my future.

Before pursuing my formal education at Southern New Hampshire University, I delved into custom designing with machine embroidery and branding for small businesses. I take pleasure in working with businesses that uphold strong morals and values.
