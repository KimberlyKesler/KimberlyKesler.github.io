Scoop Shoppe

Category: Social Media Post

The Challenge: Design an engaging social media post that captures Scoop Shoppe’s playful personality while driving online orders.

About the Project

Scoop Shoppe is an artisan creamery known for handcrafted ice creams made in small batches. The brand embraces a playful, colorful personality while emphasizing quality ingredients and inclusive options such as vegan and lactose-free varieties.

Its identity is rooted in joy, creativity, and feel-good indulgence.

Visual Strategy

The visual direction centered on bold, high-energy color and playful forms inspired by the vibrancy of ice cream itself. Bright pastels, saturated backgrounds, and joyful lifestyle photography informed the overall tone.

To reflect the brand’s quirky personality, I focused on rounded shapes, layered compositions, and dynamic framing that mimic the softness and movement of melting scoops. Repetition of circular elements and drip-inspired forms helped establish visual consistency.

The goal was to create a design language that feels expressive and fun, while remaining organized and easy to navigate within a fast-moving social media feed.

Design Development

The design development phase focused on translating the playful brand personality into a structured, cohesive layout. I explored how to balance bold color with readability, ensuring the promotional messaging remained clear within a visually energetic composition.

Hierarchy played a key role in organizing the content. Headline text was scaled for immediate impact, while supporting copy was arranged to guide the viewer naturally through the message. Rounded frames and layered elements were introduced to create depth and reinforce the soft, scoop-inspired aesthetic.

Attention was given to spacing and alignment to prevent the design from feeling overcrowded. By combining vibrant imagery with clean typography and intentional layout decisions, the composition maintains both personality and clarity.

Final Execution

The final post combines bold color contrast, layered imagery, and clear typography to create a lively and engaging composition. The melting graphic detail reinforces brand personality, while the call-to-action is positioned for immediate visibility.

The result is a vibrant promotional piece that captures the joyful spirit of Scoop Shoppe, strengthens brand recognition, and encourages customers to order and engage.
