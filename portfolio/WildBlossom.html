Wild Blossom Collective Branding Suite

About the Project

Category: Branding

Wild Blossom Collective is a community-driven organization dedicated to connecting and empowering women through accessible educational opportunities. Rooted in the belief that growth flourishes in supportive environments, the Collective provides resources that foster personal development and professional advancement.

Its mission is to equip women with the knowledge, confidence, and support needed to lead within their businesses and churches, and create lasting influence in the communities they serve.

Visual Strategy

At the beginning of the project, several concept images were provided as visual references for tone, style, and color direction. These references helped establish the overall aesthetic vision and served as a foundation for creative exploration.

Using these visual cues, a cohesive mood board was developed to define the brand atmosphere. From there, a refined color palette was created to reflect warmth and community, while typography selections balanced approachability with strength.

Identity Development

With the visual foundation established, the brand elements were expanded into a fully realized identity system. The mood board informed tone, the color palette established warmth, and typography defined the brand's voice.

These elements were refined into a scalable system designed to support growth, consistency, and real-world application across various platforms.

Comprehensive Brand System

After establishing the foundational elements, the full brand system was developed to ensure consistency and long-term flexibility. This includes logo architecture, color standards, and typography hierarchy.
